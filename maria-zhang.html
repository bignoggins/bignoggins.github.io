MARIA ZHANG

  • Founder & CEO, Alike; Sr Director of Engineering, Yahoo!
Maria is a Sr. Director of Engineering at Yahoo! Mobile. She and her team are are responsible for the iOS and Android versions of Yahoo! Homepage, Sports, Fantasy Sports and Mobile SDKs. Previously, she was the founder and CEO at Alike, a startup where mobile meets big data. Alike was acquired by Yahoo in February 2013.

She was recently named one of the Top 100 most influential women in tech by the Silicon Valley Business Journal. Earlier in her career, Maria led product development teams at Microsoft and Zillow. She studied Computer Science at Tsinghua University and E. Michigan University.
