    ABOUT US

    Inspiring and Equipping Entrepreneurs to Lead

    ELEO is unique among tech conference in the Valley. Instead of talking fundraising, pitching, or the X's and O's of running a company, we focus on something more fundamental: Leadership. We believe that every startup needs strong leadership, and that it can be learned and honed over time.

    Each year, we bring the best leaders from around the Valley together to a one-day conference. At ELEO, you won't learn theory, but practical advice from about how to actually run your company or organization. This year, we have an extremely diverse speaker lineup. You will learn from top VCs, fortune 500 CEOs, serial entrepreneurs and everyone in between.
