PAT GELSINGER

  • CEO VMware

Pat was named to become CEO of VMware Sept 2012. VMware is the worldwide leader in virtualization and cloud computing technologies with 2012 revenues of $4.5B. Going forward VMware is leading the transformation of the Software Defined Data Center’s, Hybrid Cloud computing and PC and Mobile device computing.
