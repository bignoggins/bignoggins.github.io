TIM HOWES

  • Founder & CTO, Rockmelt and Opsware; currently VP of Engineering, Yahoo!
Timothy A Howes, Ph.D., is an entrepreneur and computer scientist. His last company, Rockmelt, was purchased by Yahoo, where Tim is currently VP of Engineering for Mobile and Emerging Products. Prior to Rockmelt, Tim co-founded and was CTO of Opsware (formerly Loudcloud), sold to Hewlett Packard in 2007 for $1.65B. At HP, Tim was CTO of HP Software, HP's enterprise software division.

Prior to Opsware, he held senior positions at Netscape and AOL. Tim co-invented LDAP, the Internet directory protocol. He has written two books on directories and over 20 Internet Requests for Comment on various subjects. He has served on the boards of several public and private companies and non-profit organizations, including Blue Coat Systems, Homestead Technologies, the Internet Architecture Board, SF Jazz, and the University of Michigan College of Engineering.

Tim received his Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Michigan. He lives in Los Altos Hills with his wife, two daughters, and four dogs.
